Official Conference

The 7th International Conference on Internet Applications, Protocols and Services

(NETAPPS2024)

The Seventh International Conference on Internet Applications, Protocols and Services (NETAPPS2024) is a no-frills conference in the area of Internet communications and networking.

The main goal of this conference is to serve as an affordable platform to promote greater engagement of network researchers from around the globe, notably from the ASEAN and the Asia Pacific region, in the Internet research activities principally on those related to the work of Internet Engineering Task Force (IETF) and the Internet Research Task Force (IRTF).

Submission Guidelines

All papers must be original and not simultaneously submitted to another journal or conference. Papers should be written in English with IEEE format. Each newly submitted paper should not be more than 8 pages including figures and references.

  • check_circle Prepare your paper based on the IEEE format. Download the official IEEE Template.
  • check_circle Submit your paper online using the NETAPPS2024 Easychair account.
  • check_circle Submissions are required to be double-blind. Please remove author names and affiliations.
  • check_circle Abstract ≤ 400 words. Full paper ≤ 8 pages.
  • warning Changes in the author group and order are not accepted after acceptance.

Registration & Final Submission

Kindly follow the below guidelines carefully in order to include your paper in the conference proceedings that will be submitted to IEEE Xplore:

1
Use the IEEE template (MS Word) to prepare your camera-ready paper.
2
Prepare a Word file named ‘CorrectionsTable.docx’ listing your corrections based on Reviewer comments.
3
Revise your final camera-ready paper. Ensure it includes the affiliations and email addresses of all authors.
4
Add the copyright notice ‘979-8-3315-1951-3/24/$31.00 ©2024 IEEE’ to the bottom left corner of the first page.
5

Camera-ready Format Verification via IEEE PDF eXpress:

  • Create an account using Conference ID: 63333X
  • Verify the format and download the verified PDF file.
6
Upload the Table of Corrections, Camera Ready .docx, and Verified PDF to EasyChair by 10th October 2024.
7

By 15th October 2024, at least one author must complete the registration and payment.
